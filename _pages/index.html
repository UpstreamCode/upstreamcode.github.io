Bridging the Gap Between Technologists and Frontline Missions

In an age where Himalayan monks can be seen with the latest iPhones, Christians in the tech industry are still wondering how they can meaningfully contribute to frontier missions.

We partner with missional technologists and field teams to help advance church planting efforts among unreached language groups (ULGs). Together, we create mobile apps that bring Scripture, oral Bible stories, and music to ULGs.

Our Solution

Custom Apps for the Least of These

The unreached tend to receive less attention and resources. We’re building mobile apps that are catered to specific language groups and designing with their unique needs in mind. Our aim is to provide simple, clear, and culturally appropriate ways for people to access Scriptural content produced by locals and field teams.
