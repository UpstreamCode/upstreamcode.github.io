A Hub for Missional Technologists

As technologists, we can feel stifled when trying to use our skills to benefit our churches and Christian ministries. There often just aren’t a ton of spaces for us to serve. How can God use me for missions work when I’m wired to be a software developer?

We built Upstream to be a connection between developers and frontline missions. Put differently, we want to resolve the tension you feel as you seek to use your gifts for kingdom purposes.

Ways You Can Serve With Us

{% include svgs/icons/misc/rocket.svg %}

Solve Challenges Together

We’re building Orality Platform — a shared codebase from which multiple language-specific apps can be spun up. Join our online community and volunteer your software development, design, or product development skills.

{% include svgs/icons/misc/group.svg %}

Mentor the Next Generation

If you’re an experienced technologist, volunteer your time to mentor (technically and spiritually) younger developers and designers in our community. Your guidance and example can shape the next generation of missional technologists.

{% include svgs/icons/misc/briefcase.svg %}

Apply for a Cohort

We occasionally run 10-week cohorts for students or recent graduates. Paid interns collaborate on developing a Christian app for an unreached people group. Conducted remotely, our cohorts include group mentoring sessions led by experienced professionals and dedicated times for prayer and reflection.
