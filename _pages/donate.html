Where Does Your Giving Go?

We hire students and recent graduates for missions mentoring cohorts. These are 8-12 week cohorts of developers and designers where we:

  • Take a project from A to B. The projects make Christian material available in challenging contexts. For example, we might break ground on an app or build a specific feature for an app.
  • Mentor our interns. We hire interns who desire to use their gifts for missions. We work hard to grow our interns into what we call “missional technologists” who will carry a heart for missions with them wherever they go.

We keep this as low as possible, but we occasionally travel to meet with missionary teams and have a few paid subscriptions for the tools we use.

The vast majority of designing and coding happens through volunteers. Yet on occasion, we hire missional Christians as contractors to complete specific tasks.

We can’t thank you enough for your generosity. Upstream is committed to carefully stewarding the Lord’s resources for the sake of those who have not heard about Christ. Your donations help make the message of the Bible accessible among least reached people.
