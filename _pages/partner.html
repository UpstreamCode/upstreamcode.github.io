Any combination of your biblical recordings, text, and artwork. All contained in one app. Designed and developed solely for your people group.

Let’s Talk.

A mobile app is no silver bullet for the unreached puzzle. We understand that missionaries must evaluate their specific contexts, the particular barriers for their people groups, and what the realistic value of an app might be. However, if you think an app-shaped puzzle piece might help overcome the barriers you’re facing, we would love to start a conversation with you.

Our Process

From Idea to Reality

We want to get to know you and your ministry context.

Together with you, we hone in on project goals and what’s needed to get there.

We produce designs of the app until they’re approved by you.

Our volunteer technologists start coding. We also test for any problems.

We celebrate, give thanks, and pray as the app is made available to the public.

Make adjustments based on user feedback and project goals.
